Zombie Shooter Trick or Treat - Halloween V1.0 Amelia's Escape Build 1.0 Cave Master Caster







Once upon a time there was a wizard called Caster who had become invisible because of evil enemies who made fun of him so much that he vanished... Caster suffered from invisibility for some time until he decided to seek revenge and take back his visibility!

This game is made for "International College Jam #2".

This game uses the Unity game engine and the C# programming language.

The assets are downloaded from the Unity Asset store.

Free packages have been used to make this game.

This game was made by Sami Palomäki, Maura Ruopsa, Pauli Hamunen and Mia Parpala.
